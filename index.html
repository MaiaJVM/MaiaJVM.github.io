about me

Hello there! I am João Maia, currently a Postdoc at the Beijing International Center for Mathematical R​​​​​esearch (BICMR), Peking University, under the supervision of Professor Jian Ding. I did my Ph.D. at the Institute of Mathematics and Statistics (IME-USP), University of São Paulo, under the supervision of Professor Rodrigo Bissacot.      

      My main interests are in Classical Statistical Mechanics and Probability, with emphasys on Long-range and Random field models.
